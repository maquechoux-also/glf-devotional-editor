Missions Update

Hope for Croatia

Hope for Croatia is dedicated to bringing Biblical ideals to the secular society of Croatia — living hope to people in need, the saving love of Christ to those open to the Gospel, and His victorious Life to all who believe in Him. This mission is fulfilled through counseling, training, publishing, foster care ministries, Christian art, and the local church.

   

Please pray for God's blessing on their upcoming Christian camp, for the continued miraculous healing of their foster daughter Mauricia, and that the Lord will open more room and bring living fruit to their various ministries.
